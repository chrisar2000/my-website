⍿ About Me ⍿


⍿ Brief History ⍿

My name is Christopher Richardson. I have been involved with technology all my life. As a young teenager I took a liking to modifying the look of my Xbox 360, taking it apart, cutting out designs in the casing, applying plexi glass and internal LED's and then piecing it back together was so much fun to me. I continued to involve myself with xbox 360 projects, eventually learning to solder LED's for the ROL (Ring of Light) RF board to change the ROL colors. After some time I began to realize that many of the games I were playing then just werent enough for me. I brainstormed many ideas thinking to myself how wonderful it would be if I could make these video games a reality. I began researching how to do so and came across Unity, I quickly realized creating a video game is no easy task, even the 2D games were hard to make! I spent countless years, trail & error, learning how to code, particularly in C#. Although I may not be a mastermind, I try my best to make the kid version of me happy by creating small games that I find fun. This website is a collection of the games I have made or are still in the process of making that I find worthy.
